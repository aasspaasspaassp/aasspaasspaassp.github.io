About AASSP:

Andrés González aka AASSP (Ciudad de México, 1994) is a mexican music producer, philosopher and artist based in Mexico City. He studied Philosophy while learning how to make electronic music. As a producer and sound artist he had explored analog and digital methods to make music, focusing on the aesthetic of bass music. As a philosopher he has explored themes related to subjectivity and the way we can create new forms of desire. AASSP or Andrés (is the same) has been making various creative projects ranging from music releases, fanzines, texts and events. Currently he is learning computer science to expand his tools for making his ideas possible.
